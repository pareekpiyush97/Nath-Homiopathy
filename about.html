A Note On Homeopathic Care

Complementary, Not a Replacement

Homeopathy at Nath Homeopathy is offered as gentle, supportive care alongside — not instead of — conventional medical treatment where required. For emergencies, serious infections, or conditions needing urgent medical attention, please seek immediate specialist or hospital care. We're happy to discuss what homeopathic support may or may not be appropriate for your situation during a consultation.
